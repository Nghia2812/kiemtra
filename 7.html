Khung bài — áp dụng cho MỌI đề
Lời chào
Dear Ann / Mary / Jane / tên bạn,
Luôn dùng tên riêng, không dùng "Dear friend"
Mở đầu
(2–3 câu)
Câu 1: Vui khi nhận thư / hỏi thăm
Câu 2: Nêu lý do viết thư → dẫn vào thân bài
Cố định, học thuộc → tiết kiệm thời gian
Thân bài
(3 ý, 3 đoạn)
Đoạn 1: Ý thứ nhất của đề (gạch đầu dòng 1)
Đoạn 2: Ý thứ hai của đề (gạch đầu dòng 2)
Đoạn 3: Ý thứ ba của đề (gạch đầu dòng 3)
Mỗi đoạn: 1 câu mở + 1–2 câu giải thích + 1 câu kết
Kết thư
(2 câu)
Câu kết cố định + lời chúc
Học thuộc 1 câu dùng mãi: "Bye for now, I look forward to hearing from you soon."
Chữ ký
Warm wishes, / Love, / Best regards,  +  tên của bạn
Câu cố định — học thuộc, dùng cho mọi đề
①
Mở đầu (chọn 1)
  • I am so glad to hear from you after such a long time.
  • I hope this letter finds you well.
  • It was lovely to receive your letter.
  • How are you and your family? I hope everything is going well.
②
Dẫn vào chủ đề
  • Today, I'm writing this letter to tell you some information about [chủ đề].
  • I'm writing in response to your question about [chủ đề].
  • I'm so happy to know that [tin tức từ bạn].
  • Thank you for inviting me / asking me about [chủ đề].
③
Từ nối giữa các ý
  • Firstly, … / Secondly, … / Finally, …
  • Moreover, … / In addition, …
  • To be honest, … / Actually, …
  • Regarding [chủ đề], … / Concerning [chủ đề], …
④
Kết thư (chọn 1)
  • Bye for now, I look forward to hearing from you soon.
  • That's all for now, please write back to me soon.
  • I hope to hear from you soon. Take care!
  • Please let me know if you have any questions.
8 chủ đề hay thi — câu mẫu theo từng loại
✈
1. Bạn đến thăm VN / TP của tôi
  • I'm really happy to know you're coming to visit [city] next month.
  • Chỗ ở: You can stay with my family / at a hotel near my house.
  • Hoạt động: You can visit [địa điểm] and enjoy a lot of local food.
  • Thời tiết: The weather is hot and sunny in summer, so remember to bring T-shirts, shorts and sunscreen.
  • I'm sure you will love it here!
🌍
2. Tôi đến thăm nước bạn
  • I've never been to [country] before and I'm really excited!
  • Quà: I would like to bring a gift for your family — can you suggest something suitable?
  • Hoạt động: What activities can I do there? Can you give me some suggestions?
  • Đồ ăn: I'm keen on food — can you recommend some local dishes I should try?
  • Thời tiết: I'm also wondering about the weather and what clothes to bring.
💼
3. Tìm việc làm thêm / việc cho bạn
  • I've been accepted to [university] in London — I'm so excited!
  • Life in [city] is expensive, so I'm planning to find a part-time job.
  • Having a part-time job means I can earn money and gain valuable work experience.
  • Nhờ bạn: Since you have a wide social network, could you help me find a job as a [cashier / waitress / salesperson]?
  • Giới thiệu việc cho bạn: I found a job at [company] that might be suitable for you. To apply, visit [website].
🏠
4. Nhà mới / Chuyển thành phố
  • I have recently moved to [city/flat] to work and study.
  • It's a small but comfortable flat, about [20] square metres.
  • It's painted [blue/pink] and has [3] rooms: a living room, a bedroom and a kitchen.
  • [City] is a bustling city with many job opportunities. People here are friendly and hardworking.
  • Why don't you come and visit? I'm sure you will love it!
📚
5. Học đại học / Khóa học
  • I started studying at [university] [3 months] ago — it's a great experience!
  • I feel happy because I can make new friends and get a good degree.
  • Về khóa học: The lessons are well-designed and the teachers are experienced.
  • You can take part in many activities such as games, group work and role play.
  • Học hay đi làm: I think getting a job is better because it saves time and you earn money while learning real skills.
🏖
6. Kỳ nghỉ / Địa điểm du lịch
  • Last summer, I had a wonderful [3-day] trip to [Nha Trang / Da Lat].
  • I stayed at a [4-star] hotel by the sea — it was fully equipped and reasonably priced.
  • I had a chance to walk along the beach, see the sunrise and enjoy lots of local food.
  • My favourite was seafood — it was absolutely delicious!
  • I would highly recommend visiting [destination] — you won't be disappointed!
🙏
7. Cảm ơn / Mời ăn tối
  • Cảm ơn: Thank you so much for having me at your house for a week.
  • Without your help, I would have found it very difficult to manage.
  • I really liked the food your mum cooked — it was so delicious!
  • During my stay, I felt like a part of your family. I miss you all!
  • Mời ăn tối: Would you like to come to my house for dinner on [Sunday the 20th] at 7pm? My mum is a great cook!
  • I live about [15 minutes] from your hotel at [address]. You can take a taxi.
💬
8. Kết nối lại / Tin tức bản thân
  • I can't believe it's been so long since we last met!
  • Lately, I have been keeping busy with [work / study / new projects].
  • I recently moved to [city] and started working as a [teacher / guide].
  • Everything has changed quite a lot, but I'm happy with my life here.
  • Are you free to meet up sometime? I would love to catch up with you!

Dear tên bạn,

I am so glad to hear from you after such a long time. I'm really happy to know that you are coming to visit Vinh / Vietnam next month!

Today, I'm writing this letter to give you some useful information for your trip.

Đoạn 1 — Chỗ ở

Firstly, regarding accommodation, you can stay with my family. My parents will be very happy to meet you. If you prefer more privacy, I can book a cheap and comfortable hotel nearby.

Đoạn 2 — Hoạt động

Concerning activities, there are many things to do here. You can visit HCM Square, which is famous for its stunning architecture. Moreover, you can walk along Cua Lo beach and enjoy a lot of delicious local food.

Đoạn 3 — Thời tiết / Quần áo

Finally, regarding the weather, it is hot and sunny in summer, so I recommend you bring T-shirts, shorts and sunscreen. Don't forget to bring a camera too!

Kết thư

I'm sure you will love it here. Bye for now, I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Warm wishes,
Tên bạn

Secondly, I'm wondering about the weather and what clothes I should bring. Should I pack coats or just T-shirts? Also, what activities can I do there? I would love to try some outdoor activities and explore the city.

Đoạn 3 — Ẩm thực

Finally, I'm really keen on food and would love to try as many local dishes as possible. Can you recommend some typical food in your country that I shouldn't miss?

Kết thư

Bye for now, I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Warm wishes,

Dear tên bạn,

I hope this letter finds you well. I'm so excited because I've just been accepted to the University of London to study Marketing Management!

I'm writing to ask for your help in finding a part-time job while I'm studying.

Đoạn 1 — Kế hoạch học

I will be starting a full-time Master's course next September and the programme will last 2 years. I'm really looking forward to improving my skills and meeting new people.

Đoạn 2 — Lý do cần việc

As you know, life in London is quite expensive. To help cover my living costs and gain some work experience, I'm planning to get a part-time job alongside my studies.

Đoạn 3 — Nhờ bạn

Since you have been living in London for many years and have a wide social network, I was wondering if you could help me find a suitable position — perhaps as a cashier or waitress. I would be very grateful for any help you can offer.

Kết thư

Bye for now, I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Warm wishes,

I'm very pleased to receive your letter. I'm sorry I haven't written for so long — I've been really busy recently!

I have recently moved to a new flat / apartment. It's about 20 square metres — not very big, but it has everything I need for a comfortable life. It's painted blue and has 3 rooms: a living room, a bedroom and a kitchen.

I've moved to Vinh City, which is located in the centre of my country. It's a bustling city with many job opportunities. People here are very friendly and hardworking, which I really like.

There are also many famous places to visit here, such as HCM Square and the Central Park. Why don't you come and visit me sometime? I'm sure you will love it!

Kết thư

That's all for now. Please write back to me soon!

Your friend,

Lời chào + mở đầu

Dear tên bạn,

I'm so glad to hear from you! You asked me about a holiday I've really enjoyed — let me tell you about my trip to Nha Trang last summer!

Đoạn 1 — Đi đâu, ở đâu

Last summer, I had a wonderful one-week vacation in Nha Trang, which is one of the most beautiful beaches in Vietnam. I stayed at a 4-star hotel by the sea — it was fully equipped and very reasonably priced.

Đoạn 2 — Hoạt động

During my stay, I had a chance to walk along the beach and watch the sunrise every morning. In the evening, I hung out with my friends at a pavement café and went shopping. There were also many interesting boat trips and water sports to enjoy.

Đoạn 3 — Đồ ăn + lời khuyên

The food was absolutely amazing — my favourite was the fresh seafood, which was delicious and very affordable. I would highly recommend visiting Nha Trang. I'm sure you will have a wonderful time!

Kết thư

Bye for now, I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Love,
Tên bạn
